PubNub feature: Instant Data Transfer between mobile devices

20 February 2015 on Getting Started, Family

In 30 hours, a team of five high schoolers had prototyped their iOS application at the HackGenY hackathon earlier this month. The team’s goal was to transform the modern business card as simply as possible.

The result is Paer.

Paer is an iOS app that uses Bluetooth Low Energy Tech (BluetoothLE) to enable users to instantly exchange and transfer contact information and connect across numerous social platforms by simply touching phones. BLET is a faster and cheaper alternative to NFC, and the app essentially eliminates business cards by streamlining the process of networking.

You can easily paer with simple steps
  1. create a personal profile with your information you wish to share
  2. choose the social medias you want to use to connect with others
  3. done! go out and paer!
